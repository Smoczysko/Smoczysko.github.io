Object-Oriented Programming Languages

Materials for Object-Oriented Programming Languages course given at Gdańsk University of Technology, faculty of applied physics and mathematics in 2015/16.

Course goal is to give students brief understanding of programming in Java and it's third-one in series of courses about OO programming (from theory to practical uses in different platforms and languages).
